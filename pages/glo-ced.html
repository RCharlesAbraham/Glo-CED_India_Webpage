Glo-CED India

Glo-CED India is dedicated to character building, ethical leadership, and social transformation. We are committed to promoting holistic development from the individual to the nation. We nurture ethically grounded, value-driven, and socially responsible citizens through character building, ethical leadership, and transformative development initiatives.

Vision of Glo-CED India

To promote holistic development from the individual to the nation by nurturing ethically grounded, value-driven, and socially responsible citizens through character building, ethical leadership, and transformative development, thereby contributing to sustainable social and economic progress and inclusive nation-building.

Mission Statement of Glo-CED India

The Mission of the Trust is to empower Indian citizens and grassroots communities through comprehensive and inclusive education by fostering ethical leadership, advancing research in sustainable development, and delivering ICT-enabled capacity-building initiatives. The Trust shall function as a strategic bridge between research and policy, translating evidence-based knowledge into actionable solutions, in collaboration with Government bodies, Non-Governmental Organizations, and other stakeholders, for the effective implementation of impactful programmes in health, education, skill development, sustainable development, and livelihood enhancement, thereby contributing to the creation of a resilient, informed, and self-reliant nation.

Value Statement of Glo-CED

The Value Statement of Glo-CED defines the core principles that guide our organization's behaviour and decision-making. We stand on these four pillars:

Integrity & Ethical Leadership

We hold ourselves to the highest ethical standards, ensuring that our leadership and programs serve as a trusted model for the communities and individuals we empower.

Evidence-Based Impact

We are committed to rigorous research and data-driven solutions, ensuring our programs in health, education, and livelihood are both effective and sustainable.

Inclusivity & Empowerment

We believe in the inherent potential of every citizen and prioritize grassroots participation to ensure that development is equitable and reaches the most marginalized.

Collaborative Innovation

We embrace the power of ICT and strategic partnerships with government and NGOs to create scalable, forward-thinking solutions for a resilient nation.

Glo-CED India
