## Image Specifications

- **Format**: JPG/JPEG recommended
- **Size**: Aim for 500x600px or larger (minimum 250x300px)
- **File Size**: Keep under 200KB per image for optimal loading
- **Quality**: Use professional headshots or portrait-style photos
- **Aspect Ratio**: Portrait orientation (taller than wide)
